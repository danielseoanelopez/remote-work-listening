🎧 AUDIO 1 · THE VIDEO CALL

Sofia llama a Marcos. ¿Cuál es el problema con el cliente?

⏱ 1:18🗣 Sofia 🇬🇧 + Marcos 🇺🇸

01What does GreenMarket want?

02Why isn't the price estimate ready yet?

03What does Sofia offer to do?

🎤Speaking: What is Marcos's issue? Explain in one sentence.

MODEL ANSWERHe can't finish the price estimate because he is waiting for an update from the client.

Sofia: Hi Marcos, do you have a minute? Are you available for a quick call?

Marcos: Hi Sofia, sure, I'm available. Is everything OK?

Sofia: Yes, don't worry. It's about GreenMarket, the new client. They sent us a request this morning.

Marcos: Oh, the online store project? What do they need?

Sofia: They want a price estimate for the website, and they need it today if possible.

Marcos: OK, no problem. I already have most of the details. The price estimate is almost ready.

Sofia: Perfect. Is it attached to your email? I didn't see it.

Marcos: Not yet, sorry. There's still one task pending. I'm waiting for an update from the client.

Sofia: Ah, I see. So that's the issue.

Marcos: Exactly. As soon as they confirm, I'll finish the estimate.

Sofia: Great. Let me check that for you. I'll call them now.

Marcos: Thanks, Sofia! I'll get back to you soon with the final document.

🎧 AUDIO 2 · THE VOICEMAIL

El cliente, David, deja un mensaje de voz. ¿Qué envía y qué pide?

⏱ 0:28🗣 David 🇬🇧

01Why is David calling?

02What did David attach to the email?

03What does David ask the team to send him?

🎤Speaking: What did David send, and what does he want the team to do?

MODEL ANSWERHe sent (attached) the product list. He wants the team to confirm they received it and to send him the price estimate.
🎧 AUDIO 3 · THE CLIENT CALL

Marcos llama a David para confirmar los detalles del proyecto.

⏱ 1:15🗣 Marcos 🇺🇸 + David 🇬🇧

01What does David want to add to the project?

02What happens to the price estimate with the payment system?

03When can the team start the project?

🎤Speaking: Why is the price estimate higher now?

MODEL ANSWERBecause the client also wants an online payment system, so there is more work.

Marcos: Hi David, this is Marcos from the web team. Thanks for the product list. Do you have a minute?

David: Hi Marcos! Yes, of course. Did you get everything you need?

Marcos: Almost. I have the details, but I have one question. Do you need the online payment system too?

David: Yes, please. The payment system is very important for us.

Marcos: Perfect, that's an important detail. With the payment system, the price estimate is a little higher.

David: That's OK. Could you send me the final price estimate today?

Marcos: Of course. I'll finish it this afternoon, and it will be attached to my email.

David: Great. And when can you start the project?

Marcos: If you confirm today, we can start next Monday.

David: Excellent. I'll confirm by email in one hour.

Marcos: Thank you, David. I'll get back to you with the final document soon.
